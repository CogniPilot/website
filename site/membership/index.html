How to Join

Apply for membership

Email the foundation with enough context for the membership discussion to start cleanly.

  1. Send a note to membership@cognipilot.com with your organization name, primary contact, and the membership level you want to discuss.
  2. Briefly describe how your organization expects to use, support, or contribute to CogniPilot and open safety-oriented autonomy infrastructure.
  3. The foundation will follow up on fit, level, contribution expectations, and the next steps for onboarding.

Membership levels

Members participate at Platinum, Gold, Silver, and Copper levels. Support level is currently assessed heuristically based on a variety of factors, including developer contributions, hardware contributions, and other forms of support. Founding members are recognized below and on the mission page.

Why membership matters

CogniPilot is infrastructure for teams that want autonomy software to be open, inspectable, verifiable, and shared across organizations instead of rebuilt in isolation.

Membership gives sponsors and research partners a structured way to support that work and help guide the foundation roadmap.
