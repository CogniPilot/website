01 / WHY

Three principles, no compromise

// 01

Open

A completely open autopilot platform for academia, government, and industry alike. No vendor lock-in, no proprietary black boxes, and no path for it to ever fall under the control of a for-profit company.

// 02

Secure

Minimum viable code for the mission means a smaller attack surface and fewer lines to verify. Worst-case attacks are modeled in the reachable set, so security is proven with the same math as safety. Trustworthy from silicon to sky.

// 03

Concise

Mathematically modeled control with provable robustness. Small, auditable, fully integrated, and deliberately built to be understood, not just trusted.

Our most fundamental value is a commitment to a completely open autopilot platform, steered by the nonprofit CogniPilot Foundation, and never influenced by any for-profit company, present or future.

02 / PLATFORM

One brain, five regions

CogniPilot is architected like the human brain: each subsystem is a specialized region, from Corti reasoning like the cortex down to Cerebri handling reflexes like the cerebellum. Together they form a full autonomy pipeline from perception to actuation.

Corti

Behavior & planning

ML-based behavior, path planning, dynamic mission adaptation, and complex task execution.

Pari

Payloads & fusion

ML for payloads, multi-sensor fusion, and real-time spatial awareness.

Oculi

Vision systems

Collision avoidance, visual-inertial odometry, and onboard perception.

Tempi

Comms & connectivity

Communication, telemetry, and cloud connectivity.

Cerebri

Flight firmware

Controls, fail-safes, and manual override running on the Zephyr RTOS.

03 / TOOLCHAIN

Proofs become the code

Most autopilots are written by hand and tested after the fact. CogniPilot inverts that. We model the vehicle and its controller in Modelica, prove the safety properties on that model, and our Rumoca compiler generates the flight code directly from it, so the proof and the running firmware can never drift apart.

STEP 01

Model

MODELICA

The vehicle dynamics and control law are described as equations: one precise, physics-level source of truth.

STEP 02

Prove

INVARIANTS

Robustness and safety invariants are proven directly against the Modelica model, not a hand-written approximation of it.

STEP 03

Rumoca

COMPILER

Rumoca compiles the verified model straight to autopilot code. The proof and the source are generated from the same artifact.

STEP 04

Deploy

ZEPHYR RTOS

Cerebri runs the generated code on Zephyr, honoring the proven model as a binding flight-time contract.

Modelica model → formal proof → Rumoca compiler → Zephyr autopilot MODEL = CONTRACT
04 / SAFETY

Provable safety, not best‑effort.

CogniPilot targets a class of assurance not currently available in other open-source autopilots, enough to conduct safety-critical missions like public transport. It is not a drop-in for hobby drones; it is the foundation for the next generation of certified autonomy.

  • ✓Accurate mathematical models of vehicle & control software
  • ✓Mathematically provable robustness properties
  • ✓Templates for out-of-tree custom vehicles
  • ✓Fail-safes and manual override at the firmware layer
